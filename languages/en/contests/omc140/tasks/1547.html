CMO-kun is participating in a contest of OMC, but he can't understand any of the problems no matter how much he thinks, so he would like to at least guess the answer to Problem A correctly.
What CMO-kun knows about Problem A is the following two points:

  • The answer is a positive rational number that is less than 111 and its 204820482048 times is an integer.
  • When expressing the answer as p/qp/qp/q using coprime positive integers ppp and qqq, he must submit p+qp+qp+q as the answer.

In this case, CMO-kun has decided to submit the possible values of positive integers he should answer, in increasing order, exactly once each until he submits a correct answer (CA). Please find the maximum number of submissions CMO-kun could make for Problem A. Note that once he submits a CA, he will not make any further submissions, and we do not consider the submission limit of 101010 here.
