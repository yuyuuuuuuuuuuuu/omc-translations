There is a pile consisting of nnn identical stones. Using this, two players, TKG and Shoko, play a game. They take turns removing stones from the pile according to the following rules. When both players do their best to win, please calculate the sum of integers nnn such that Shoko wins for integers between 111 and 100010001000 inclusive.

  • The number of stones that can be taken at once can be either 1,3,4,5,6,7,8,91, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 91,3,4,5,6,7,8,9 (note that 222 is specifically excluded).
  • As long as there are stones in the pile, both players continue to take stones, and the person who takes the last stone wins.
  • TKG will take the first stone.
