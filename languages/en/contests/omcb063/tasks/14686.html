Write distinct positive integers from 111 to 101010 at each vertex of a regular 101010-gon, and define the score as follows:

  • The sum of the greatest common divisors of the two numbers written at the endpoints of the 101010 edges.

Let the maximum possible score be MMM. How many ways are there to write the numbers such that the score is MMM?
Note that arrangements that are identical due to rotation or reflection are considered distinct.
