A 4-digit positive integer NNN is a palindrome that can be expressed as the product of three distinct prime numbers less than or equal to 20. Please answer the total sum of possible values for NNN.
A positive integer is considered a palindrome if it does not end with 000 and reads the same forwards and backwards. For example, 122112211221 and 388338833883 are palindromes, while 202320232023 and 121012101210 are not.
