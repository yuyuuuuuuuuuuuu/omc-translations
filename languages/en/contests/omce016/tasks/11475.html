Given three integers x,y,zx, y, zx,y,z and two positive odd numbers M,NM, NM,N, where x,y,zx, y, zx,y,z are coprime, and

x2+yz,y2+zx,z2+xyx^2 + yz, y^2 + zx, z^2 + xyx2+yz,y2+zx,z2+xy
form an arithmetic sequence with common difference NNN in that order, and furthermore
Mxyz=210⋅257NMxyz = 2^{10} \cdot 257NMxyz=210⋅257N
holds, please find the sum of all possible values of NNN.

About "Coprime" Multiple integers a1,...,ana_1, ..., a_na1​,...,an​ (not necessarily positive) are said to be coprime if the only positive integer that divides all of a1,...,ana_1, ..., a_na1​,...,an​ is 111.
