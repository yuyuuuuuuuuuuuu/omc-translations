Find the sum of all positive integers NNN that satisfy exactly three of the conditions A,B,C,DA, B, C, DA,B,C,D (i.e., satisfy exactly one condition not).

  • Condition AAA: NNN is less than or equal to 100010001000.
  • Condition BBB: NNN is a multiple of 111111.
  • Condition CCC: The sum of the digits of NNN in decimal representation is 111111.
  • Condition DDD: NNN is a multiple of 333.
