There are five transparent boxes, A,B,C,D,EA, B, C, D, EA,B,C,D,E, that can hold up to 505050 balls each. Each box currently contains between 000 and 494949 balls. Using these five boxes, Sugita-kun and Nakamura-kun took turns playing a game with the following operation:

  • Select at least one box and add one ball to each of the selected boxes.

Sugita-kun goes first, and the person who cannot make a move loses the game. There are a total of 50550^5505 combinations of the number of balls at the start of the game, but how many combinations exist such that Sugita-kun can win regardless of how Nakamura-kun plays?
