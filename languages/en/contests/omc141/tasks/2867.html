OMC has nnn candies. Each of these candies has a different weight, ranging from 1,2,…,n1, 2, \ldots, n1,2,…,n (in grams). OMC wants to distribute these candies to three people, O-kun, M-kun, and C-kun, such that the total weight of the candies each person receives is equal. How many suitable integers nnn are there between 111 and 100010001000 inclusive, for which this distribution is possible?
