There are 101010 Othello pieces arranged in a row, all facing up with the black side up and the white side down. By repeatedly performing the following operations, you can create all 2102^{10}210 possible board configurations from this state.

  • In the odd-numbered operations, select one Othello piece and flip that piece and all pieces to the right of it.
  • In the even-numbered operations, select one Othello piece and flip that piece and all pieces to the left of it.

For each of the 2102^{10}210 possible board configurations, calculate the sum of the minimum number of operations required to achieve that configuration. Note that the minimum number of operations required to achieve the initial state where all pieces are facing up is considered to be 000.
