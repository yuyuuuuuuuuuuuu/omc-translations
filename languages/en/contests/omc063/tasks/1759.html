Please find a 10-digit positive integer NNN that satisfies all the following conditions. Note that such an NNN exists uniquely.

  • Each digit of NNN appears exactly once from 000 to 999. Furthermore, the most significant digit of NNN is not 000.
  • For any k=1,2,⋯ ,10k=1,2,\cdots, 10k=1,2,⋯,10, the integer obtained by cutting out the top kkk digits of NNN is divisible by kkk.

For example, the integer obtained by cutting out the top 333 digits of 123451234512345 is 123123123.
