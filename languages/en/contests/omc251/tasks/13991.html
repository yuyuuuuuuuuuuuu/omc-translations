Find the number of integer pairs $(a_1,a_2,\ldots a_{15},b_1,b_2,\ldots,b_{15})$ that satisfy the following conditions:

  • For $1\leq i\leq j\leq 15$, if $a_i=a_j$ and $b_i=b_j$, then $i=j$ holds.
  • For $k=1,2,\ldots,14$, the condition $|a_k-a_{k+1}|+|b_k-b_{k+1}|=1$ holds.
  • $a_1=b_1=1$ and $a_{15}=b_{15}=7$.
