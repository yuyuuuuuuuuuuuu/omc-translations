OMC-kun is on the cell with the multiplication table that has 111 written on it. He wants to move to the cell that has 818181 written on it while satisfying the following condition and in the shortest distance. How many ways can he move at this point?

  • He can only move to cells that have 111, 818181, or an even number written on them.

Furthermore, the multiplication table refers to a grid of 9×99 \times 99×9, where the cell in the iii-th row and jjj-th column contains the product i×ji \times ji×j.
