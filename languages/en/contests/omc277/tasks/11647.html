There are 808080 cards, each with an integer written on it ranging from 000 to 797979. We want to find the number of ways to divide these 808080 cards into 202020 groups of 444 cards each, such that for each group, the numbers on the 444 cards can be rearranged to form an arithmetic sequence, and at least one of the following conditions holds:

  • The remainders when the integers on the 444 cards are divided by 202020 are all the same.
  • The remainders when the integers on the 444 cards are rearranged can form an arithmetic sequence with a common difference of 111.

Note that the order of the 202020 groups is not considered.
