A point where both the xxx coordinate and the yyy coordinate are odd is called an odd point, a point where both are even is called an even point, and a point that is neither is called a mixed point. Since we need to pass through odd points 444 times, we must start by passing through either (1,1)(1,1)(1,1), (1,3)(1,3)(1,3), or (3,1)(3,1)(3,1). There are 222 ways to get from (0,0)(0,0)(0,0) to these points, and similarly, there are 222 ways from the last odd point to (6,6)(6,6)(6,6).
