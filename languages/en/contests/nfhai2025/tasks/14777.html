A positive integer NNN that satisfies all of the following conditions is called a mild number.

  • The decimal representation of NNN consists only of the digits 0,3,5,60, 3, 5, 60,3,5,6 (it is allowed for some digits not to be used).
  • The decimal representation of NNN contains at least one of the digits 333 or 555.
  • NNN is not a multiple of 555.
