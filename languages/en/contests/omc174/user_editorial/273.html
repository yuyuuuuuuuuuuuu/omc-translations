In the official explanation, the expressions (1)(1)(1) and the ways to choose nnn colors can be understood by labeling the vertices arbitrarily with 1, …, 2n1,\, \ldots,\, 2n1,…,2n, and considering the selection of vertices connected by edges for the youngest unselected vertex each time, which results in (2n−5)!!, (2n−1)!!(2n - 5)!!,\ (2n - 1)!!(2n−5)!!, (2n−1)!! respectively (it can be seen that this is equivalent even if the expressions in the official explanation are rearranged). Using this reduces the number of calculations slightly.
