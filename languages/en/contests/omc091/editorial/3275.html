The problem is equivalent to the following expression.

  • In how many ways can we add either 0,3,40, 3, 40,3,4 a total of 555 times so that the sum is at least 555?

Conversely, considering how many ways there are for the sum to be less than 555, this is equivalent to using 333 and 444 at most 111 time combined, which gives us 5×2+1=115 \times 2 + 1 = 115×2+1=11 ways. From this, the value we seek in the original problem is 35−11=2323^{5} - 11 = \textbf{232}35−11=232 ways.
