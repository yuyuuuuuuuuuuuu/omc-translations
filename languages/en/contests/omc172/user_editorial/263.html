There are probably various counting methods. I will describe a method that differs from the official explanation.
Like the official explanation, we interpret the problem as coloring each cell in black and white, such that each color forms a puzzle. For the 4×44×44×4 grid, we denote the cell in the xxx-th row and yyy-th column as (x,y)(x,y)(x,y), and we will refer to the four points (2,2),(2,3),(3,2),(3,3)(2,2),(2,3),(3,2),(3,3)(2,2),(2,3),(3,2),(3,3) as internal and the other cells as external (the terms internal and external are the same as in the official explanation).
