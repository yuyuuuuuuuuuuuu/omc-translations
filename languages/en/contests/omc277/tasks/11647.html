There is one card for each of the 808080 integers from 000 to 797979. In how many ways can these 808080 cards be partitioned into 202020 bundles of 444 cards each so that, for every bundle, the four numbers on those cards can be rearranged to form an arithmetic progression, and moreover at least one of the following conditions holds?

  • The remainders of the integers on the 444 cards when divided by 202020 are all equal.
  • The remainders of the integers on the 444 cards when divided by 202020 can be rearranged to form an arithmetic progression with common difference 111.

　Here, the order of the 202020 bundles is not taken into account.
