There are 444 distinguishable boxes arranged in a row, and their arrangement is fixed. A total of 10410^4104 indistinguishable balls are placed in these boxes. Given the following conditions, how many combinations of the number of balls in each box are possible?

  • For any two distinct boxes, the number of balls in the left box is less than the number of balls in the right box.
  • The total number of balls in the left two boxes is 201920192019.

Note that it is allowed for some boxes to contain no balls at all.
