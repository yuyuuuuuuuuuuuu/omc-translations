There is a 10×1010 \times 1010×10 grid. How many ways can you color the grid with black and white so that the following condition is satisfied?

  • Condition: In every 2×22 \times 22×2 sub-grid, there must be exactly 222 white squares and 222 black squares.

Here, a 2×22 \times 22×2 sub-grid refers to the intersection of adjacent rows and adjacent columns, consisting of 444 squares.
