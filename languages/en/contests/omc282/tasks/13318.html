There is a warehouse shaped like a grid with 100010001000 rows and 222 columns, and this warehouse can be entered and exited only from the 222 cells in the 111st row. OMC-kun is considering transporting the items 1,2,…,20001, 2, \dots, 20001,2,…,2000 into the warehouse in this order according to the following conditions.

  • OMC-kun will take one item from outside the warehouse and enter the warehouse through either of the two entrances, move to a cell that does not yet have an item placed in it, place the item in that cell, and then exit the warehouse.
  • He cannot move to a cell that already has an item placed in it.
  • Once an item is placed, it cannot be moved again.

Let NNN be the total number of possible configurations of the items after all 200020002000 items have been placed. Find the maximum number of times NNN can be divided by 222.
