  • The deck is divided into packets from packet 111 to packet ddd. Packet iii consists of the iiith card, the (i+d)(i+d)(i+d)th card, and so on.
  • Within each packet, all cards are reversed, and by stacking packet i+1i+1i+1 on top of packet iii, the deck is reconstituted.
