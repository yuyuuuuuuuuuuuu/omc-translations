In a grid with 333 rows and 888 columns, we write one non-negative integer in each cell. Here, integers from 111 to 888 are used exactly once, and the remaining 161616 cells contain only 000. In this case, how many ways can we fill the grid such that the following condition is satisfied?

  • For any cell that does not belong to the leftmost column, the number written must be at most twice the number written in the cell to its left.

Furthermore, arrangements that are identical through rotation or reflection are considered distinct.
