Assign a positive integer to each face of a cube and then assign numbers to all edges and vertices according to the following rules:

  • For each edge, assign the product of the two numbers assigned to the two faces that share that edge.
  • For each vertex, assign the product of the three numbers assigned to the three faces that share that vertex.

After assigning numbers to all faces, edges, and vertices in this way, the total of the 26 assigned numbers is distinct, and the sum of these 26 numbers is 1154.
Let \( S \) be the sum of the largest and smallest of the 8 numbers assigned to the vertices. Please find the total sum of all possible values of \( S \).
