By considering the operations in reverse order, this problem can be rephrased as follows.

  • There are 161616 distinguishable stones lined up in a row. Choose one stone that has an even number of stones (including 000) to its right, remove it, and perform this operation 161616 times to remove all the stones. What are the possible sequences of operations?

The answer to this is easily calculated as (8!)2=1625702400(8!)^2=\bm{1625702400}(8!)2=1625702400 ways.
