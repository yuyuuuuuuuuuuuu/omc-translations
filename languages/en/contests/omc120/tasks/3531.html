Around a round table, nnn people (denoted as person 111, person 222, ..., person nnn in a counterclockwise manner) have prepared one gift each for a gift exchange party. Initially, each person i=1,…,ni=1,\ldots,ni=1,…,n only has the one gift they prepared themselves.
During the gift exchange, all participants will simultaneously perform the following exchange 555 times.

  • Exchange: If person iii currently has the gift prepared by themselves, they will pass it to the iii-th person by counting counterclockwise (starting with their neighbor).

However, if at the end of any exchange there is a person who has 222 or more gifts, the gift exchange will be considered a failure and no further exchanges will take place. How many values of nnn, where 2≤n≤3142 \leq n \leq 3142≤n≤314, can complete all 555 exchanges without failure?
