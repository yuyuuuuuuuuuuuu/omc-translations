How many ways are there to choose 555 lattice points, where both xxx and yyy coordinates are between 111 and 101010, without duplication, such that the following condition is satisfied?

  • From the chosen 555 lattice points, there is exactly 111 way to select 222 different points such that the midpoint of those 222 points is also a lattice point.

Note that a lattice point refers to a point where both xxx and yyy coordinates are integers.
