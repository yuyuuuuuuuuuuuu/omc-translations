How many positive integers nnn between 111 and 100010001000 satisfy the following condition?

  • There exists a positive integer kkk such that the last 3 digits of nkn^knk and nk+1n^{k+1}nk+1 are the same.

Note that for a positive integer xxx with less than 333 digits, the last 3 digits of xxx are defined as xxx padded with 000s to make it 3 digits (for example, the last 3 digits of 222 are 002002002).
