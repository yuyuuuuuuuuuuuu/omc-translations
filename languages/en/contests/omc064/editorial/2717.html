The number of people staying in the circle regardless of operations is always even. In particular, when everyone is divided alternately into two groups, it is easy to see that an even number of people are eliminated from the circle from each group. Therefore, it is not possible to complete the operation in one go.
On the other hand, it can be completed in two operations, so let's count this.
