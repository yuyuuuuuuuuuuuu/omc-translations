On the blackboard, the arrangement of 1,2,⋯ ,1001, 2, \cdots, 1001,2,⋯,100 is written in a single row, and person A repeats the following operation 500050005000 times.

  • For all pairs of adjacent 222 numbers, write the sum of those 222 numbers between them.

At this time, there are 100!100!100! ways to arrange the numbers initially written on the blackboard (considering that arrangements that are mirror images are distinguished), and for all of them, the average of the number of odd numbers when the operation is finished is an integer. Therefore, please find the remainder of this value when divided by the prime number 500350035003.
