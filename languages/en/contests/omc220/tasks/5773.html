There is a grid of 2×14682 \times 14682×1468. In this case, how many ways can we fill each cell with the integers 1,2,3,41, 2, 3, 41,2,3,4 such that the following conditions are satisfied?

  • Adjacent cells contain different integers.
  • There are exactly 444 locations where the sum of the integers written in adjacent 222 cells is not equal to 555.
