The mathematics research club at TKG High School has a total of 777 members, and OMC-kun serves as the president. One day, OMC-kun received a box of cookies containing 151515 bags from an alumnus of the club. These will be distributed to all members, ensuring that each member receives at least 111 bag, while OMC-kun himself intends to receive at least 222 bags. In this case, how many ways are there to distribute the cookies to each member?
Note that the different bags of cookies are considered indistinguishable.
