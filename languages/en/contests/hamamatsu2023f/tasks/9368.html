In triangle ABCABCABC where AB>ACAB > ACAB>AC and AB>BCAB > BCAB>BC, there is a point DDD on line segment BCBCBC (excluding the endpoints). Additionally, there is a point EEE on the arc BCBCBC of the circumcircle of triangle ABCABCABC (excluding the endpoints) that does not contain point AAA, satisfying BD=DEBD = DEBD=DE. When a line through point DDD that is parallel to line ABABAB intersects segment AEAEAE (excluding the endpoints) at point PPP, we need to show that AP=CPAP = CPAP=CP holds true.
