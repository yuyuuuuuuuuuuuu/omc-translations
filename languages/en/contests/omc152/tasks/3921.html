The OMC country consists of 121212 islands, and each pair of distinct islands is connected by exactly one bidirectional bridge. The Mochi-kun decided to color these bridges in red, green, or blue, subject to the following conditions:

  • For each color CCC, there exist distinct islands I1I_1I1​ and I2I_2I2​ such that it is not possible to reach I2I_2I2​ starting from I1I_1I1​ by crossing only the bridges colored CCC.

  • For every island, there exists at least one other island that is connected by a bridge colored red.

Please determine how many ways there are to color the bridges such that the number of bridges colored red is maximized. Note that all islands are considered distinct.
