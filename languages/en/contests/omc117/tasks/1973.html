In the xyxyxy plane, a special light ray is emitted from a lattice point PPP that is not on the axes as follows:

  • It travels a total distance of 151515 without dissipating, and disappears at the point when it has traveled a distance of 151515.
  • It reflects in such a way that the angle of incidence equals the angle of reflection, according to the xxx-axis and yyy-axis.
  • However, if it is incident at the origin, it reflects in the same direction as the incident direction.
    In this case, it is considered to have reflected once on each axis, totaling 222 reflections.

Under these conditions, how many lattice points PPP satisfy the following condition?

  • A light ray that reflects 222 times on the axes and disappears at a lattice point that is not on the axes has a unique direction of emission.
