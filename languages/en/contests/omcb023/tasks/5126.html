It is known that there are finitely many natural numbers that satisfy the following two conditions:

  • Have exactly 567856785678 positive divisors.
  • Have 567856785678 as a divisor.

Let the product of all such natural numbers be PPP; determine the number of positive divisors of PPP.
Note that the prime factorization of 567856785678 is 5678=2×17×1675678=2 \times 17 \times 1675678=2×17×167.
