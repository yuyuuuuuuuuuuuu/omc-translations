There are NNN piles of stones arranged in a row, each containing 000 or more stones. For k=1,2,…,Nk = 1, 2, \ldots, Nk=1,2,…,N, the pile in the kkk-th position from the left initially contains kkk stones. Santa and the reindeer play the following game using these NNN piles:

  • Santa plays first and they take turns making the following moves as long as possible:
    • Select either 111 or 222 piles and take 111 or more stones from each (when selecting 222 piles, it is allowed to take a different number of stones from each). It is not allowed to select a pile with 000 stones, but it is permissible for a pile to have 000 stones as a result of the move.
  • The player who cannot make a move loses, and the other player wins.

When both players take actions with the goal of winning optimally, a positive integer NNN for which Santa, who plays first, wins is called a good number. Please calculate the sum of all good numbers that are less than or equal to 202320232023.
