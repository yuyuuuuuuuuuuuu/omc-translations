There are 202020 squares arranged in a single row, and in each square, one of the numbers 1,2,3,4,51, 2, 3, 4, 51,2,3,4,5 must be selected and written. How many ways are there to write the numbers while satisfying the following conditions? Note that it is acceptable to leave some numbers unwritten, and palindromic arrangements are considered distinct.

  • The sum of the numbers written in any consecutive 555 squares must always be a multiple of 555.
