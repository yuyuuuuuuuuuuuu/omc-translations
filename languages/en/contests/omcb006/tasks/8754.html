How many ways can we place a total of 444 pieces, consisting of 222 white pieces and 222 black pieces, on a 5×55\times 55×5 grid with 111 piece in each of 444 squares, such that the following two conditions are satisfied?

  • There is no column with 222 white pieces placed.
  • There is no row with 222 black pieces placed.

Note that pieces of the same color are indistinguishable.
