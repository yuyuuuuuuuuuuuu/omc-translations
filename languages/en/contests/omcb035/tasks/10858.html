Takagi-kun and OMC-chan climb a staircase with nnn steps. Takagi-kun can take 111 step or 888 steps in one move, while OMC-chan can take 111 step or 666 steps only. Both want to climb the stairs with as few steps as possible. How many possible positive integers nnn can make the number of steps taken by Takagi-kun and OMC-chan the same?
