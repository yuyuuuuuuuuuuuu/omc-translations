There are 100100100 cards, each colored with one of the 252525 different colors labeled 1,1,1, 2,2,2, …,\ldots ,…, 252525 (not all colors need to be used). Cards that are colored the same are indistinguishable. You will play a game with OMC-kun using these 100100100 cards as follows:

  • OMC-kun divides the 100100100 cards into 252525 piles of 444 cards each.
  • You choose 111 card from each pile, for a total of 252525 cards.
  • If the 252525 cards chosen are all of different colors, you win; otherwise, OMC-kun wins.

Regardless of OMC-kun's actions, how many ways can the cards be colored such that you are guaranteed to win? Note that the coloring is considered different only when there exists a color with a different number of colored cards.
