Mixtilinear Incircle and Its Basic Properties The circle that is tangent to the sides ABABAB and ACACAC of triangle ABCABCABC and the circumcircle is called the mixtilinear incircle of triangle ABCABCABC with respect to vertex AAA. Let the points of tangency of this circle with the circumcircle of triangle ABCABCABC and the sides ABABAB and ACACAC be denoted as SSS, TTT, and UUU, respectively, and let the circumcircle and incenter of triangle ABCABCABC be denoted as ω\omegaω and III. The following properties hold:

  • The line ISISIS passes through the midpoint of the arc BCBCBC of ω\omegaω that contains AAA.
  • The line ITITIT passes through the midpoint of the arc ABABAB of ω\omegaω that does not contain CCC.
  • The line IUIUIU passes through the midpoint of the arc ACACAC of ω\omegaω that does not contain BBB.
  • The lines AIAIAI and STSTST are perpendicular at III.
  • The quadrilaterals SBTISBTISBTI and SIUCSIUCSIUC are similar.
