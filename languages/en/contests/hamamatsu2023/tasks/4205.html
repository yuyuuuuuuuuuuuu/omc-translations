You are to make a hamburger by stacking exactly 101010 layers of ingredients (bread, patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato) under the following conditions:

  • The top and bottom layers must be bread.
  • It must contain at least 111 patty.
  • No two adjacent layers can be the same ingredient.
  • It is acceptable to leave some ingredients unused.

How many different ways can this hamburger be made?
Note that the top and bottom are considered distinct, and the same ingredient should not be distinguished.
