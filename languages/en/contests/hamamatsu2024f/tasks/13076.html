The mini Hamamatsu City consists of 7 districts from District 111 to District 777. Each district has a positive real number area not exceeding 111, and the total area sums to 555. The mayor is considering reorganizing the districts of mini Hamamatsu City as follows:

  • Choose integers 1≤k<l≤61\leq k\lt l\leq 61≤k<l≤6, and merge Districts 111 to kkk, Districts k+1k+1k+1 to lll, and Districts l+1l+1l+1 to 777 to form three new districts.

In this case, regardless of the areas of the districts, the mayor claims that with proper reorganization, the area of any new district can be made at least CCC. Find the maximum possible value of such a real number CCC. Note that having exactly one district remain as the new district is also considered a merger.
