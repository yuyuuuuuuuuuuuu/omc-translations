There are nnn square-shaped LED boards connected in a circular manner as shown in the figure below (the figure is for n=15n = 15n=15). These LED boards can be independently turned on and off, and initially, all are off. Additionally, there exists a template of the same shape with nnn squares, where each square is painted either white or black.
Let us consider n=30n = 30n=30. In this case, there are 2302^{30}230 ways to paint the template, but how many of these can be transformed into any arrangement of on and off states on the LED boards (which also has 2302^{30}230 possibilities) by repeating the following operation a certain number of times?
Note that both the LED boards and the template are considered distinct when arrangements are created by flipping or rotating them.

Operation. After rotating the template as desired (flipping is not allowed), align the template with the LED boards so that the squares match, and simultaneously toggle the on/off states of all LED boards that overlap with the black squares of the template.
