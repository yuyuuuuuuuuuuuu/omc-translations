In a 5×55\times 55×5 grid, how many paths exist from the point at the bottom left to the point at the top right that move along the edges in the shortest way (that is, passing through 101010 edges) while satisfying the following condition?

  • The absolute difference in the number of cells contained in the two regions created by the path should be a composite number.

Note that when the grid is divided into 000 cells or 252525 cells, it is also considered as "divided into 2 regions."
Here, a composite number refers to an integer that is not a prime number and is greater than or equal to 222.
