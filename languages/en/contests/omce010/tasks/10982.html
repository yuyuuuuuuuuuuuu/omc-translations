There is a rectangle grid with 555 rows and 202020 columns, and initially, all the squares are painted white. The number of ways to paint 000 or more of the squares in the first row black is 2202^{20}220, but how many of these ways satisfy the following condition?

  • It is possible to cover all white squares completely without overlaps, gaps, or overflow using 1×21\times 21×2 rectangles.

Note that the 1×21\times 21×2 rectangles can be rotated.
