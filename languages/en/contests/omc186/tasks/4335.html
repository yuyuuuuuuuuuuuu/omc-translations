  • From any city, it is possible to reach any other different city by using the drawn roads while possibly passing through several cities, and there exists exactly one route such that no city is visited more than once.
  • There are at most 111 road for each city among cities 1,…,10001, \ldots, 10001,…,1000.
  • There are at most 222 roads for each city among cities 1001,…,20001001, \ldots, 20001001,…,2000.
  • There are at most 333 roads for each city among cities 2001,…,30002001, \ldots, 30002001,…,3000.

Given that M3000!=pq\dfrac{M}{3000!} = \dfrac{p}{q}3000!M​=qp​ for coprime positive integers ppp and qqq, please provide the remainder of p×qp \times qp×q when divided by 300030003000.
