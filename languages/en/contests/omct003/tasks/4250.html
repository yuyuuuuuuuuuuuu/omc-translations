There is an 8×88\times88×8 grid, and initially, every cell contains the number 111. Taro and Jiro alternately performed the following operations, starting with Taro, for a total of 500050005000 times each:

  • Taro chooses one of the rows with the highest sum of numbers and changes all the cells in that row to 000.
  • Jiro chooses one of the columns with the lowest sum of numbers and changes all the cells in that column to 111.

After all operations are completed, please find the total sum of the numbers written in the entire grid.
It is guaranteed that the value to be found is uniquely determined, regardless of the operations performed.
