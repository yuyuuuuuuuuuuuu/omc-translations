Using integers a,b,ca, b, ca,b,c between 000 and 999, express the sum of finite decimals in the form 0.abc0.abc0.abc that cannot be represented as finite decimals in both binary and quinary, in decimal notation. Note that in the representation 0.abc0.abc0.abc, it is acceptable for the number to end with 000, such as 0.1000.1000.100.
