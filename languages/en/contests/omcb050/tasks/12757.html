There are 999 people standing on the circumference, and each person chooses a favorite integer between 111 and 999. How many ways can they choose such that there exists an integer nnn between 222 and 888 that satisfies the following condition?

  • Regardless of how nnn consecutive people are chosen on the circumference, the sum of the integers they chose is the same.

Note that the 999 people are distinguishable.
