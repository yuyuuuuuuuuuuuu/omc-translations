OMC decided to color each cell of this grid either white or black. However, (1,1)(1,1)(1,1) and (101,101)(101,101)(101,101) must always be colored white. There are a total of 2101992^{10199}210199 possible coloring configurations, and for each of them, solve the following problem and find out the maximum number of times the total MMM of the answers can be divided by 222:

  • Problem: CMO is currently at cell (1,1)(1,1)(1,1) and is attempting to move to cell (101,101)(101,101)(101,101) by repeatedly moving to an adjacent white cell either to the right or downward. How many different possible paths can CMO take? Note that two paths are considered different if the set of cells they pass through is different.
