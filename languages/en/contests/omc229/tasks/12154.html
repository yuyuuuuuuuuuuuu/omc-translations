On the xyxyxy plane, there is a piece PPP at (0,0)(0,0)(0,0) and a piece QQQ at (0,8)(0,8)(0,8). The operations AAA, BBB, and CCC are defined as follows.

  • Operation AAA: Move the piece at (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) straight to (x+2,y)(x+2,y)(x+2,y).
  • Operation BBB: Move the piece at (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) straight to (x+1,y+1)(x+1,y+1)(x+1,y+1).
  • Operation CCC: Move the piece at (x,y)(x,y)(x,y) straight to (x+1,y−1)(x+1,y-1)(x+1,y−1).

When performing operations AAA and BBB on piece PPP, and operations AAA and CCC on piece QQQ, in any order (a combination of unused operations is allowed), the goal is to determine how many combinations of paths exist such that the length of the common part of the paths taken by pieces PPP and QQQ is exactly 222 when they are each moved to (24,8)(24,8)(24,8) and (24,0)(24,0)(24,0) respectively.
