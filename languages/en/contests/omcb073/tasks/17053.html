How many ways are there to arrange the cards numbered 1,2,3,4,5,61,2,3,4,5,61,2,3,4,5,6 in a row such that the following condition is satisfied?

  • By swapping the positions of pairs of cards that have numbers which are not coprime, zero or more times, the numbers on the cards can be arranged in the order 1,2,3,4,5,61,2,3,4,5,61,2,3,4,5,6 from left to right.
