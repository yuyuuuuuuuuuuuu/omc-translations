Let one of the vertices of the regular dodecahedron be XXX, and let YYY be the vertex that is symmetric to XXX with respect to the center.
Snail-kun starts at XXX and moves to adjacent vertices for a total of 10410^4104 moves. Note that it is possible to return to the vertex that was just occupied. We are asked to find the total number of movement methods that satisfy the following conditions, modulo the prime number 500350035003:

  • Never returning to XXX and reaching YYY for the first time on the 10410^4104th move.

Moreover, movement methods that can be aligned by rotation are also counted as different methods.
