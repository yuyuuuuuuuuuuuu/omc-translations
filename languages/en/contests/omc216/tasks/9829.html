There are 999 white and black stones lined up in a row, and three people, A,B,CA, B, CA,B,C, perform the following operation in the order of A,B,C,A,B,C,…A, B, C, A, B, C, \ldotsA,B,C,A,B,C,…

  • Select one stone from either end (left or right) and remove it.

By performing operations until there are no stones left, AAA and BBB can work together to ensure that the total number of black stones taken by CCC is always 111 or less, regardless of CCC's choices. How many possible initial arrangements of stones satisfy this condition?
Note that the initial arrangement of stones can have colors that are not used.
