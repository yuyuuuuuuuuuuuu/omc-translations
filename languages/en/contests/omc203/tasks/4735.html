A rectangle with all four sides of odd length is called a good rectangle. We will fill a 7×77 \times 77×7 grid with several rectangles, each side length being an integer (not necessarily good rectangles). Find the total sum of possible values for the number of good rectangles used among the filled rectangles. Note that a square is considered a type of rectangle.
