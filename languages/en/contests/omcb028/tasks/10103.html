There are 101010 cards, each with one integer written on it from 111 to 101010, with each integer being used exactly once. When these cards are distributed among AAA, BBB, and CCC without any leftovers, the following conditions hold:

  • The sums of the numbers on the cards held by AAA and BBB are equal.
  • The product of the numbers on the cards held by BBB is divisible by 161616.
  • CCC has 444 cards and the sum of the numbers on them is at least 303030.

Find the total possible values for the product of the numbers on the cards held by AAA.
