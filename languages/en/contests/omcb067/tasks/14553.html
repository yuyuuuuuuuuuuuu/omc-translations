There are 333 distinguishable universities and 888 distinguishable high schools in OMC City. OMC City, which wants to promote collaboration between high schools and universities, recommends that high schools and universities collaborate. How many ways are there to establish collaboration between high schools and universities that satisfy the following conditions?

  • All high schools collaborate with 111 or 222 universities.
  • All universities collaborate with 222 or more high schools.

Collaboration between high schools and universities is assumed to be bidirectional. Also, collaboration between high schools and between universities is not considered.
