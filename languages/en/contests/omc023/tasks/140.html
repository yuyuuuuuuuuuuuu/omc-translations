Torii and Torio are going to compete with dice. Specifically, they will each roll a die that shows equal probabilities of landing on faces numbered from 111 to 666, and the one who rolls the higher number will win. However, if they roll the same number, it will be a draw, and there will be no winner.
In this case, please find the probability that Torii wins.
Please express the answer in terms of positive integers aaa and bbb such that the greatest common divisor is 111, and give the sum a+ba+ba+b as the answer.
