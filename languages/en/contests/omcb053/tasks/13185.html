There are 141414 citizens in the OMC country, numbered 1,2,…,141,2,\dots,141,2,…,14, and each citizen lives in either the Honest Village or the Liars Village. The following conditions hold:

  • Residents of the Honest Village always tell the truth.
  • Residents of the Liars Village always lie.
  • Each citizen knows who lives in the Honest Village and who lives in the Liars Village.

Now, if we line up the 141414 citizens in order of their numbers 1,2,…,141,2,\dots,141,2,…,14 and ask each resident, "Is at least one of your two neighbors (for the person at the end, only one neighbor) a resident of the Liars Village?" and everyone answers "Yes", how many combinations of villages can be considered for these 141414 people?
