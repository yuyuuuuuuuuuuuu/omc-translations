Let's consider a situation where a silver general of shogi is placed on the xyxyxy plane. When the silver general is at the point (x,y)(x, y)(x,y), after one move, it can move to one of the following points:

(x−1,y+1), (x,y+1), (x+1,y+1), (x−1,y−1), (x+1,y−1) (x - 1, y + 1),~ (x, y + 1),~ (x + 1, y + 1),~ (x - 1, y - 1),~ (x + 1, y - 1)(x−1,y+1), (x,y+1), (x+1,y+1), (x−1,y−1), (x+1,y−1)
Initially, the silver general is placed at (0,0)(0, 0)(0,0). Moving forward 15 times, there are 5155^{15}515 possible ways, but how many of these result in the general being located at (10,11)(10, 11)(10,11)?
