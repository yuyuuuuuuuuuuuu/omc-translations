For a quadrilateral RRR with vertices at lattice points on the Cartesian coordinate plane, we define its score as follows:

R の辺上の格子点の個数+2×(R の内部にある格子点の個数)R\ \text{の辺上の格子点の個数} + 2\times(R\ \text{の内部にある格子点の個数})R の辺上の格子点の個数+2×(R の内部にある格子点の個数)
Note that the interior of RRR does not include points on the edges.
Let SSS be the square with vertices at the points (0,0),(101,0),(101,101),(0,101)(0,0),(101,0),(101,101),(0,101)(0,0),(101,0),(101,101),(0,101). We consider selecting one lattice point from each of the 444 edges of SSS (excluding endpoints) to form a quadrilateral. There are 1004100^41004 possible such quadrilaterals, but we need to calculate the total score for all of them.
