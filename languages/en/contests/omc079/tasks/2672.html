There is a grid with mmm rows and nnn columns, where we place 1×31 \times 31×3 blocks in such a way that they do not overlap and do not extend beyond the grid. Rotation is allowed. In this case, there exists a placement such that exactly one of the mnmnmn squares remains empty, and the possible empty squares are exactly m+nm+nm+n places. Find all ordered pairs of positive integers (m,n)(m,n)(m,n) for which this occurs, and calculate the sum of mnmnmn for these pairs.
