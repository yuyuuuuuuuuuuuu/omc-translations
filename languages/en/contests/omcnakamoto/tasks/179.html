The answers to the Nakamoto Cup problems are not necessarily all integers. If the answer is an integer, please provide that integer as your answer. If the answer is not an integer, it will be a value that can be expressed as a fraction with both the numerator and denominator being integers. In that case, express the answer as an improper fraction that cannot be simplified further, and arrange the numerator and denominator in that order to form a single integer-like answer.

  • If the answer is 555, respond with 555.
  • If the answer is 23\dfrac{2}{3}32​, respond with 232323.
  • If the answer is 171217\dfrac{1}{2}1721​, respond with 352352352.
  • If the answer is 1.3751.3751.375, respond with 118118118.
