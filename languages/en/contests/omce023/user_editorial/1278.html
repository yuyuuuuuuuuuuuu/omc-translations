From the formula in the official explanation (b+4)x2−bm2=8b+53 (b+4)x^2-bm^2=8b+53 (b+4)x2−bm2=8b+53 it might be easier to transform it into the form U2−DV2=C U^{2}-DV^{2}=C U2−DV2=C. This form is known as the Pell Equation, and you might understand it better by reading the following article. It may not be very helpful, but it's just for reference...

https://mathworld.wolfram.com/PellEquation.html

When D>0 is not a perfect square, this equation always has non-trivial integer solutions, and you can generate infinitely many solutions from the smallest solution (the fundamental solution).
