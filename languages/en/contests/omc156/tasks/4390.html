Let the total number of pieces placed in the iii-th row from the top be denoted as RiR_iRi​, and the total number of pieces placed in the jjj-th column from the left as CjC_jCj​. The ordered tuple (R1,R2,…,R13,C1,C2,…,C11)(R_1,R_2,\dots,R_{13},C_1,C_2,\dots,C_{11})(R1​,R2​,…,R13​,C1​,C2​,…,C11​) is called a characteristic tuple. Now, if the piece arrangement can be uniquely determined from the characteristic tuple, that arrangement is called a good arrangement. Furthermore, in any arrangement of pieces (whether it is good or not), a piece that can be removed such that the remaining arrangement is good is called a good piece.
