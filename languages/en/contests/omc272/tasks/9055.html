A string of length 100100100 consisting only of A,B,CA, B, CA,B,C is called a good string if A,B,CA, B, CA,B,C appear exactly 20,30,5020, 30, 5020,30,50 times, respectively. The complexity of a string SSS consisting only of A,B,CA, B, CA,B,C is defined by the following value.

  • For all ways to choose two distinct AAA in SSS, let the number of BBB and CCC between those two AAA be bbb and ccc, respectively. The complexity is the sum of b2c2b^2 c^2b2c2 for all such pairs.

In this case, please find the arithmetic mean of the complexities of all good strings.

Example For example, the complexity of the string S=ABCABCAS = ABCABCAS=ABCABCA is
12⋅12+12⋅12+22⋅22=181^2 \cdot 1^2 + 1^2 \cdot 1^2 + 2^2 \cdot 2^2 = 18 12⋅12+12⋅12+22⋅22=18
as calculated.
