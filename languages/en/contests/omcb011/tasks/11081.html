In the cube ABCD−EFGHABCD - EFGHABCD−EFGH, the line segment AGAGAG is the space diagonal (the longest diagonal that passes through the interior of the cube). How many ways are there to move from vertex AAA to vertex GGG, only along the edges, without retracing any path and not passing through the same vertex more than once?
However, the starting point AAA and the destination point GGG are also counted as being passed through at the time of departure and arrival.
