200020002000 boxes are arranged in a circular pattern. The number of ways to fill each box with one of a red ball, blue ball, or white ball, satisfying the following conditions when viewed counterclockwise:

  • The box next to the one with a red ball contains either a red ball or a blue ball.
  • The box next to the one with a blue ball contains either a blue ball or a white ball.
  • The box next to the one with a white ball contains either a white ball or a red ball.

Let the total number of configurations that satisfy these conditions be MMM. Note that configurations that are identical under rotation or reflection are considered distinct. Please find the remainder of MMM when divided by the prime number 200320032003.
