If the parity of the hundreds place and the units place matches, the suitable tens place is uniquely determined. The cases where both are odd amount to 525^252 ways, and the cases where both are even (noting that the units place can be 000) amount to 4×54\times 54×5 ways, leading to a total of 45\mathbf{45}45 ways. Moreover, since the common difference can range from −4-4−4 to 444, it is permissible to count each case.
