The cuboctahedron is a solid obtained by performing the following operation at each vertex of a cube:

  • For each vertex, consider the plane passing through the midpoints of the 333 edges that have that vertex as an endpoint, and truncate the vertex side along this plane.

Masa wants to write numbers on the cuboctahedron dice. First, he writes the integers from 111 to 888 on the triangular faces one by one, and then he writes the product of the 444 numbers on the adjacent triangular faces on each square face. As a result, there are exactly 101010 different integers written on the dice. Please find the maximum possible value of the integers that can be written.
