There is a rod of length 202120212021. This rod has division points at intervals of length 111 from the ends, and it can be freely bent at these points. How many ways are there to choose 222 division points such that the following condition is satisfied?

  • Condition: When the rod is bent at the 222 division points, it can form a triangle by bringing the two endpoints of the rod together.

Note that all division points are distinguishable, and the length at the division points is considered to be negligible.
