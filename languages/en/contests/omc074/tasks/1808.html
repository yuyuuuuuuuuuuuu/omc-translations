Let nnn be an integer between 222 and 100100100. Using nnn squares arranged in a row and 100100100 pieces, Mr. Adachi and Mr. Shimamura will play a game as follows.
At the beginning, all pieces are placed in the leftmost square. Mr. Adachi goes first, and they take turns repeating the following operation a number of times between 222 and 999 (inclusive) as they like:

  • Select one piece that is not in the rightmost square and move it to the square immediately to the right of its current position.

The player who cannot correctly perform their turn first loses, and the other player wins.
In this case, please find the sum of nnn such that Mr. Adachi can definitely win if he plays his best.
