There is a grid of 204720472047 rows and 204820482048 columns, where 204720472047 black stones and 204720472047 white stones are placed. However, no more than one stone can occupy the same cell. The way to place the stones such that there is at most 111 of each color in each row and at most 111 of each color in each column is counted as NNN ways.
Let aaa be the number of times NNN is divisible by 222, and bbb be the number of times NNN is divisible by 333. Find the value of ababab.
Note that arrangements that are the same under rotation or flipping are considered different.
