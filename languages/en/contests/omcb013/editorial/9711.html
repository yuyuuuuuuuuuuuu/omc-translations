In the former case, person CCC will be singing in the order of x,y,zx,y,zx,y,z or z,x,yz,x,yz,x,y, while in the latter case, person CCC will sing in the order of x,y,zx,y,zx,y,z. Since there are 666 different ways for person AAA to sing, the total number of cases is 6×3=186 \times 3 = \mathbf{18}6×3=18 ways.
