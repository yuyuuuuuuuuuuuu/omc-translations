We will write integers from 111 to 666 six times in a 6×66 \times 66×6 grid. In this case, we define the score for each writing method as follows:

  • For each row, each column, and each diagonal, we find the remainder when the sum of the 666 written numbers is divided by 777, and the score is the sum of all 141414 such numbers.

In this case, please calculate the (additive) average of the scores for all possible writing methods. The answer should be expressed as ab\dfrac{a}{b}ba​ where aaa and bbb are coprime positive integers, and you should provide the value of a+ba+ba+b.
Note that writing methods that coincide through rotations or reflections are treated as distinct.
