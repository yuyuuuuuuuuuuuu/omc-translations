Let the prime number p=109+7p = 10^9 + 7p=109+7, and define N=4p+7N = 4p + 7N=4p+7.
There is a country consisting of NNN cities, and initially, there are no bridges between any two of the 222 cities. Each of the NNN cities has 111 cat, and all the cats simultaneously start the following movement:

  • Choose one city that can be reached by crossing exactly 111 bridge from the current city and take exactly 111 hour to move to that city (they do not turn back along the way).

Here, it is assumed that multiple cats cannot cross on a single bridge, nor can there be multiple cats in the same city. At this time, let the number of ways to construct a total of N+1N+1N+1 bridges between cities meet the following conditions be MMM:

  • Each bridge connects a different pair of cities, and there is at most 111 bridge between any two different cities.
  • Each city has at least 111 bridge connected to it.
  • Movement can be carried out continuously for NNN hours.

Please provide the remainder of MMM when divided by the prime ppp.
